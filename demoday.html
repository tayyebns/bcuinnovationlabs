The Day

Demo Day is the culmination of everything Cohort I has built across nine workshops. Participants will pitch their ventures live to a panel of industry professionals, investors, and university stakeholders.

Alongside the pitching session, Innovation Fest will feature a startup expo where each team showcases their venture — products, prototypes, and ideas at various stages of development.

The event is free and open to the public. Whether you're a student, professional, potential collaborator, or just curious — come and see what the next generation of Birmingham founders has built.
