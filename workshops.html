Programme structure

Eight working sessions. One public finish.

BCU Innovation Labs runs across eight structured sessions, followed by a public Demo Day at Innovation Fest. Each session focuses on a key pillar of early-stage venture development so that students build momentum in the right order, rather than trying to improvise everything at once.
